Iberic Burgers

Who Are We?

At Iberic Burgers, we combine the gastronomic tradition of Aracena with a modern and gourmet approach.

Our dishes are inspired by the rich local cuisine, with a special emphasis on Iberian pork, a symbol of our region and the protagonist of our recipes.

Our mission is to offer a unique experience where authenticity and innovation meet in every bite. From our burgers to our desserts, each dish reflects the passion and respect for traditional flavors.

We invite you to explore our culinary proposal and enjoy an experience that blends the best of tradition with a contemporary touch.

Points of Interest

Castle of Aracena

A medieval fortification atop a hill, offering spectacular views of the Sierra de Aracena. A trip to the past full of history and unique landscapes.

Cave of Wonders

An impressive natural cave in Aracena with stalactites, stalagmites, and inner lakes. A unique destination for nature and geology enthusiasts.
